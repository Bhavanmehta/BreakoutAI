The Read — 3 layout samples

Same content, three arrangements. The goal: make "The Read" shorter/wider so it stops towering over News & Sentiment. Scroll through and pick one.

Sample 1 — 3-up row

One row above the chart: Breaking-out verdict · Edge vs Risk · News. Most compact vertically; columns get narrow on smaller screens.

Sample 2 — Read full-width, News below

The Read spans full width with Verdict (left) / Edge vs Risk (right). News & Sentiment becomes its own full-width card underneath. Roomiest for the two Read halves.

Sample 3 — Split Read, News stays right

Keeps the current [Read | News] two-column row, but splits The Read internally into Verdict-left / Edge-vs-Risk-right. Closest to today; the two Read halves are the most cramped.
